About Bee Good Honey

Bee Good Honey has been founded to join other forces in the world to help preserve bees and wild pollinators across the globe and to help to prevent adulterated honeys entering the market.

We believe that by working with beekeepers to fund research through the products they sell we can further extend the good work they do.

We are working hard to create a world standard in honey, a trademark that can be traced right back to the person that extracted the honey no matter where they are in the world.

By buying from Bee Good Honey, you can be confident you are making a positive difference to the long term survival of bees.
